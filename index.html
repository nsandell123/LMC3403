How to Set Up MFA with Duo

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) adds an extra layer of security. Follow these steps to set it up:

  1. Log into your Georgia Tech account.
  2. Go to the "Security Settings" section.
  3. Enable Duo Mobile and follow the instructions to link your phone.

Case Study: Australian National University (ANU) Breach

In 2018, the Australian National University (ANU) experienced a sophisticated cyberattack where hackers accessed 19 years' worth of sensitive personal data, including names, birth dates, addresses, and phone numbers. The attack required no user interaction, relying on advanced techniques to bypass defenses and remain undetected for months. This incident highlights the vulnerabilities academic institutions face due to the volume of sensitive data they manage and the importance of robust cybersecurity measures to prevent such breaches.

Spot Phishing Attempts

Watch for the following signs of phishing:

  • Emails from unknown senders.
  • Urgent requests for personal information.
  • Poor grammar or spelling mistakes.
  • Links that lead to fake websites.

Creating a Strong Password: Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s

  • Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Create passwords at least 12 characters long.
  • Use a password manager.

Don’ts

  • Reuse passwords across sites.
  • Include personal details like your name.
  • Write passwords down in insecure locations.
